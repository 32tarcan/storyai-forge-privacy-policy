Privacy Policy - StoryAI Forge

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to StoryAI Forge ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you use our app and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases or subscribe to premium features, we process transaction data through Apple's App Store. We do not directly collect or store your payment information.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with our app, including:
    • Stories you generate
    • App features you use
    • Time spent in the app
    • App performance data
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device, including:
    • Device model
    • Operating system version
    • App version
    • Language preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To process your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • To improve our app and user experience
  • To provide customer support
  • To detect and prevent technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your stories and personal data are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Subscription Management

Subscriptions are managed through Apple's App Store. You can manage and cancel your subscription through your Apple ID settings. Subscription cancellation and refund policies are subject to Apple's terms and conditions.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions
  • Apple App Store: For processing payments

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing of your personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing of your personal data

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

  • Email: support@storyaiforge.com

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These rights include:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect about you
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. If you are located outside the United States and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the data to the United States and process it there.

13. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Our app does not use cookies directly. However, our third-party service providers may use tracking technologies to improve the functionality of their services.

15. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
